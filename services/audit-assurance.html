Professional Audit & Assurance Services

N. Betharia & Associates provides comprehensive audit and assurance services to ensure your business maintains the highest standards of financial transparency and regulatory compliance. Our experienced team conducts thorough audits following the Standards on Auditing (SA) issued by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

Audit & Assurance
Statutory & Internal Audit Services

Why Choose Our Audit Services?

  • 25+ Years Experience: Proven track record in audit and assurance
  • ICAI Compliance: All audits follow prescribed standards
  • Industry Expertise: Experience across multiple sectors
  • Timely Delivery: Meeting all statutory deadlines

Our Audit Services Include:

Statutory Audits

Mandatory audits for companies as per Companies Act, 2013. Comprehensive examination of financial statements and internal controls.

Tax Audits

Income Tax Act compliance audits for businesses meeting specified turnover criteria. Detailed examination of tax compliance.

Internal Audits

Systematic evaluation of internal controls, risk management, and governance processes to improve operational efficiency.

GST Audits

Goods and Services Tax compliance audits to ensure proper GST implementation and reporting.

Our Audit Process:

1
Planning & Risk Assessment

Comprehensive understanding of your business and identification of audit risks.

2
Fieldwork & Testing

Detailed examination of transactions, documents, and internal controls.

3
Reporting & Communication

Clear audit reports with findings, recommendations, and management letters.
