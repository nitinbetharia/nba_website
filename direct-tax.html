Direct Tax Consulting

Comprehensive Income Tax Advisory and Compliance Services

Our direct tax consulting services cover comprehensive tax planning, compliance, and representation before tax authorities. We help clients navigate the complexities of the Indian tax system and optimize their tax positions while ensuring full compliance with regulatory requirements.

Tax Planning & Advisory
  • Strategic tax planning for individuals and businesses
  • Investment and expense planning
  • Salary structuring and perquisite planning
  • Capital gains optimization
Income Tax Compliance
  • Income tax return filing for all categories
  • Advance tax calculations and payments
  • TDS/TCS compliance and reconciliation
  • Form 15G/15H and other exemption certificates
Tax Audit Services
  • Tax audit under Section 44AB
  • Transfer pricing documentation and audit
  • International taxation compliance
  • Special audit assignments
Representation & Litigation
  • Assessment proceedings representation
  • Appeals before CIT(A), ITAT, and Higher Courts
  • Search and seizure matters
  • Penalty and prosecution defense
