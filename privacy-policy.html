Privacy Policy

At N. Betharia & Associates, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all visitors to our website and our clients, in accordance with the guidelines issued by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) and the Code of Ethics for Chartered Accountants.

Information Collection and Use

  • We collect only such personal information as is necessary for the purpose of providing professional services or responding to your queries.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data unless required by law or for the performance of our professional duties.
  • Any information collected through forms on this website is used solely for the purpose stated and is not shared with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Confidentiality

  • All information provided by clients is treated as strictly confidential and is protected under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949 and ICAI Code of Ethics.
  • We do not disclose client information to any third party without prior consent, except as required by law or regulatory authorities.

Cookies & Tracking

  • This website may use cookies for technical purposes only. No personal information is collected via cookies.

Links to Other Sites

  • Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such sites.

Compliance with ICAI Guidelines

  • This website is intended to provide general information about our firm and services, and does not constitute solicitation or advertisement in violation of ICAI guidelines.
  • We adhere strictly to the ICAI Code of Ethics and all applicable rules regarding the use of websites by Chartered Accountants in India.
