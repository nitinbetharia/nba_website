Our practice areas

We are a leading audit firm rendering comprehensive professional services to large corporates, small and medium business houses and individuals alike.

Our services include Independent Audit & Assurance, Tax Consulting, Managment Consulting, Accounting and Corporate Support, Personnel Recruitment, Legal and Secretarial Support, Tax Audit and Advice o­n Direct and Indirect Taxes.

Audit & Assurance Services

We provide various Auditing & Assurance Services to Companies and Firms and Trusts. Audit includes thorough verification of evidences, vouchers & certifications. The audits are conducted as per the Standards on Auditing issued by ICAI.

Income Tax Advisory

We provide Income tax consultancy and compliance services with proper tax planning. We provide income tax advice to companies, LLPs, partnership firms, trusts and individual clients. We assist to design, structure and implement tax planning.

Goods and Service Tax (GST) Advisory

We provide complete end-to-end GST advisory & Compliances services including registrations, returns, tax advisory & GST Audits to businesses in India.

Outsourced Accounting & Payroll services

Outsourcing help companies to focus on their core activities and also achieve high level of compliance. We provide accounting outsourcing services using Tally.
